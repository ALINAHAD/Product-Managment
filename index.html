Product Case Study – Funnel & A/B Test

A complete data-driven product analysis project

Overview

This project demonstrates end-to-end Product Management analysis using Funnel Metrics and A/B Testing. It includes data generation, funnel breakdown, experiment design, insights, and feature recommendations.

Conversion Funnel

A/B Test Comparison

Key Insights

  • The largest drop-off occurs during the signup step (~32% conversion).
  • A/B test difference was not statistically significant.
  • Funnel issues show high friction on the signup flow.
  • Purchase conversion is strong once users sign up.

Product Recommendations

  • Reduce signup friction (shorter form, social login, autofill).
  • Run more aggressive UI/UX experiments.
  • Conduct usability testing to identify friction points.
  • Increase A/B experiment sample size.
